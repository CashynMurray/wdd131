Fly Fishing Collective - Site Plan

Site Name

Fly Fishing Collective

This name reflects a community-driven site where fly fishers can share their favorite rivers, fishing stories, and photos. The word "Collective" emphasizes collaboration and shared experiences.

Site Purpose

This website serves as a community hub for fly fishing enthusiasts. Users can explore shared fishing locations, post photos of their catches, and submit their own favorite spots. It encourages storytelling, discovery, and connection through the shared passion of fly fishing.

Scenarios

  • What are some great fly fishing spots near me that others recommend?
  • How can I share my latest fly fishing trip, photos, and tips?

Color Schema

  • #2a9d8f (Teal Green): Used for headings, buttons, and navigation highlights.
  • #f4f9f4 (Light Cream): Used for page backgrounds to create a clean, natural feel.

Typography

  • Headings: 'Playfair Display', serif – to give a classic outdoor magazine vibe.
  • Body Text: 'Segoe UI', sans-serif – clean and readable for paragraphs and general content.
